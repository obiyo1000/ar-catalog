💡 What is LA-60SE? — This is a sample processing automation system, not an analyzer. It handles pre-analytical steps: bulk loading, centrifugation, serum quality check, and aliquoting. Then routes prepared samples to connected analyzers (chemistry, immunoassay, hematology, coagulation).
📌 Ideal for: Medium to large hospitals, reference labs, and diagnostic centers looking to automate pre-analytical workflows. Perfect for labs experiencing high sample volume and seeking ISO 15189 accreditation with standardized processes.
